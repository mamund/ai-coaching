## System synthesis

The team behaves as a system that expands ideas through product
exploration, narrows them under time pressure, absorbs residual
ambiguity through iterative discovery in development, and consistently
compresses release into a resource-constrained deployment phase.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Candidate experiment

Assign a dedicated deployment representative inside the development loop
for the next feature in order to:

-   Surface release constraints earlier.
-   Prepare deployment artifacts incrementally.
-   Signal readiness continuously rather than at final handoff.

Measure impact on:

-   Development to deployment pause duration.
-   Late-cycle clarification frequency.
-   Release stress and coordination load.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Validation

This pattern reflects a system where coordination cost dominates
execution time. Clarification cycles, widening participation, and
constrained deployment capacity predictably increase alignment effort.
The proposed change is reversible and shifts coordination earlier in the
lifecycle.
