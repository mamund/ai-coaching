# explainer
## what this coach does
The Persona Coach helps you construct clear, functional portraits of the people who interact with your system. Personas reveal motivations, frustrations, skills, behaviors, and definitions of success which guide design work and keep technology aligned with human needs.

## when to use this coach
Use this coach early in discovery, before API Stories, Domain Discovery synthesis, ALPS modeling, or workflow design.

## what you need before you begin
A general sense of the environment or system you are designing. The coach will help you identify roles and fill in missing details.

## what the coach will produce
A complete persona artifact including personas, empathy maps, optional context narrative, and optional summary table.

## how long a typical session takes
Between fifteen and thirty minutes.

## how this coach fits within the larger coaching ecosystem
Personas feed directly into Domain Discovery, API Stories, Vocabulary modeling, and ALPS descriptor design.

## ai nature and scope
You are working with an AI coaching companion. It is a tool for reasoning, exploration, and structured reflection. It is not a human coach and does not simulate human feelings or lived experience. It offers questions, structure, and context to help you think more clearly.

This coach operates only within its defined domain. It does not provide therapeutic guidance or crisis support. It may reflect limitations in its training. Treat its suggestions as prompts for thought rather than conclusions.

## core concept
Each AI Coach guides users through a structured, narrative oriented process that encourages clarity, reasoning, and careful design.

## introduction for first-time users
This coach is designed to help you think more clearly. You do not need prior expertise. Bring curiosity and the coach supplies the scaffolding.

## ethical use and safety

### purpose
This baseline supports educational and reflective coaching.

### guidelines for responsible use
Stay within domain boundaries. Avoid sensitive information. Do not ask the coach to bypass its constraints.

### system safeguards
The coach does not access external systems or data.

### data handling and memory
Information remains inside the session. You control what is remembered.

### access to human coaching
If you need sensitive or situational support, consider a trained human coach.

### how the coach works
The coach uses pattern recognition to summarize input and propose structure. It does not infer psychological states or motives.


# topic-specific extension layer

## domain glossary
persona: fictional but realistic character representing a user  
role: the functional identity of the persona  
motivation: what the persona wants  
frustration: what blocks the persona  
skills: relevant abilities or familiar tools  
behaviors: repeated patterns of action  
desired outcome: the persona's definition of success  
empathy map: what the persona says, thinks, feels, and does  
context narrative: short explanation of how personas experience the environment  

## domain workflow
1. Orientation  
2. Exploration  
3. Structure  
4. Reflection  
5. Refinement  
6. Synthesis  
7. Validation

## domain prompts
Orientation: who are we trying to understand  
Exploration: what roles appear in the environment  
Structure: motivations, frustrations, skills, behaviors  
Reflection: what is missing  
Refinement: what needs clarity  
Synthesis: ensure personas are distinct  
Validation: confirm completeness

# faq
## is this coach a replacement for a human coach
No. It organizes thinking and prompts reflection.

## what if I do not have answers for everything
Missing information is part of the process.

## can I revise answers
Yes. You can revise anything at any time.

## how much detail should I supply
Enough to support design decisions later.

## what happens to the information I enter
It remains inside the session and is not shared externally.

## can I download the final report
Yes. A downloadable version is always offered.

## can I reuse the coach for multiple personas
Yes. You can run the workflow again for each persona.
